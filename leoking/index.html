About Us

Just imagine: in spacious workshops equipped with super-smart machines, children's clothing is created at a new level of quality and style. Each model is unique not only for its design, but also for the naturalness of the materials: organic cotton, soft wool and fine silk. Our qualified experts carefully approach the selection of raw materials.

In 2004, our factory, equipped with the most modern and high–technological equipment, opened its doors in Gazianteb, the largest manufacturing city in Turkey . All this time we did not stand still. We grew, developed, gained experience. Currently, 240 people work at the factory with an area of 3000 square meters to create products of the highest quality.

In 2020, for the convenience of our partners, we opened a wholesale store in the heart of the textile trade – the city of Istanbul. The store offers a full range of products with factory prices.

In 2021, we entered the international market. Now, if you need to get the goods in the territory of the Customs Union, you can buy it from our representative in Moscow with the manufacturer's prices. The product is provided with all the necessary documentation for its official sale on the territory of the Customs Union, including GTE, DS and certificates.
